Japanese Animation

Paragraph about anime

Japanese animation of any kind is known as anime, you may find that most people have a favorite anime, typically Naruto, Dragon Ball Z, One Piece or One Punch Man.

Shonen

An action based genre that primarily details characters powers and abilities in a long storyline.

Shojo

A genre of Japanese comics and animated films aimed primarily at a young female audience.
